# NovaDesk Knowledge Base

## SECTION 1: PRODUCT OVERVIEW

NovaDesk is a cloud-based customer support platform designed for
growing businesses. It centralizes customer conversations across
email, live chat, and social media into a single inbox. Teams can
collaborate on tickets, automate repetitive tasks, and measure
support performance through built-in analytics.

NovaDesk is built for support teams of all sizes — from solo
founders handling their own support to enterprise teams with
hundreds of agents.

## SECTION 2: PRICING PLANS

NovaDesk offers three pricing plans:

### Starter Plan — $29/month
- Up to 3 agents
- Email support channel only
- 1,000 tickets per month
- Basic reporting (last 30 days)
- Community support only
- No integrations

### Pro Plan — $79/month
- Up to 15 agents
- Email + live chat + social media channels
- Unlimited tickets
- Advanced reporting (last 12 months)
- Priority email support (24hr response)
- Up to 10 integrations
- Custom ticket fields
- Saved replies (macros)

### Enterprise Plan — $199/month
- Unlimited agents
- All channels including WhatsApp and SMS
- Unlimited tickets
- Full reporting with custom dashboards
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone + email support
- Unlimited integrations
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Custom SLA rules
- Data export and API access

All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required
for trial. Annual billing gives 20% discount on all plans.

## SECTION 3: BILLING AND ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

### Upgrading a Plan
You can upgrade your plan at any time from Settings > Billing.
The upgrade takes effect immediately. You will be charged a
prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.

### Downgrading a Plan
Downgrading is allowed once per billing cycle. The downgrade
takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle, not
immediately. If you downgrade from Pro to Starter, you will
lose access to live chat, social media channels, and integrations
at the end of the current billing cycle.

### Cancelling a Subscription
You can cancel at any time from Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan.
After cancellation, your account remains active until the end of
the current billing period. After that, your account is locked
and data is retained for 60 days before permanent deletion.

### Refund Policy
NovaDesk does not offer refunds for partial months. Annual plan
subscribers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.

### Changing Payment Method
Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods to add or change your
card. NovaDesk accepts Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
PayPal is not supported.

## SECTION 4: TEAM AND AGENT MANAGEMENT

### Adding Agents
Agents can be added from Settings > Team > Invite Agent. An
invitation email is sent to the agent. The invitation expires
after 48 hours. On the Starter plan, you cannot add more than
3 agents. Attempting to add a 4th agent will prompt you to
upgrade to Pro.

### Agent Roles
NovaDesk has three agent roles:

- **Admin**: Full access to all settings, billing, and reports.
  Can add or remove agents and change roles.
- **Agent**: Can view and respond to tickets. Cannot access
  billing or team settings.
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to tickets and reports. Cannot
  respond to tickets or change any settings.

### Removing an Agent
Admins can remove agents from Settings > Team. Removed agents
lose access immediately. Their previously handled tickets remain
in the system and are reassigned to the admin who removed them.

### Agent Availability
Agents can set their status to Available, Busy, or Away. When
set to Away, tickets are not auto-assigned to that agent.
Live chat is only routed to agents with Available status.

## SECTION 5: TICKET MANAGEMENT

### Creating a Ticket
Tickets are created automatically when a customer sends an email
to your support address or initiates a live chat. Tickets can
also be created manually by agents from the New Ticket button.

### Ticket Status
Tickets can have the following statuses:
- **Open**: Awaiting agent response
- **Pending**: Awaiting customer reply
- **Resolved**: Issue marked as solved
- **Closed**: Resolved tickets auto-close after 7 days of
  inactivity, or can be closed manually

### Ticket Assignment
Tickets are auto-assigned using round-robin by default. Admins
can change assignment rules from Settings > Automation.
Tickets can also be manually assigned or reassigned by any agent.

### Ticket Priority
Agents can set ticket priority to Low, Medium, High, or Urgent.
Urgent tickets appear at the top of the queue and trigger
email notifications to all admins.

### Merging Tickets
Agents can merge duplicate tickets. The older ticket becomes
the primary. All replies from both tickets are combined.
The merged ticket cannot be unmerged.

## SECTION 6: LIVE CHAT

### Setting Up Live Chat
Live chat is available on Pro and Enterprise plans only.
To enable, go to Settings > Channels > Live Chat and copy
the chat widget code into your website's HTML.

### Chat Routing
Incoming chats are routed to Available agents only. If no
agents are available, the customer sees an offline message
and can leave their email for a follow-up ticket.

### Chat Hours
You can set business hours for live chat from Settings >
Channels > Live Chat > Business Hours. Outside business hours,
the chat widget shows an offline form automatically.

### Chat Transcripts
All chat transcripts are saved automatically as tickets.
Customers can request a copy of their transcript via email
from the chat window.

## SECTION 7: INTEGRATIONS

### Available Integrations
Pro plan supports up to 10 integrations. Enterprise plan
supports unlimited integrations. Starter plan has no
integrations.

Current integrations include:
- Slack (receive ticket notifications in Slack channels)
- Jira (create Jira issues from tickets)
- Salesforce (sync customer data)
- Shopify (view order details inside tickets)
- Zapier (connect to 5000+ apps)
- GitHub (link tickets to GitHub issues)
- HubSpot (sync contacts and deals)
- Google Analytics (track chat widget performance)

### Setting Up an Integration
Go to Settings > Integrations, choose the integration, and
follow the setup wizard. Most integrations require OAuth
authorization with the third-party service.

### Removing an Integration
Go to Settings > Integrations > Manage, and click Disconnect.
This immediately stops data sync but does not delete
historical data already synced.

## SECTION 8: REPORTING AND ANALYTICS

### Available Reports
- Ticket Volume: Total tickets by day, week, or month
- Response Time: Average first response and resolution time
- Agent Performance: Tickets handled and satisfaction scores
- Channel Breakdown: Ticket sources by channel
- Customer Satisfaction (CSAT): Ratings from resolved tickets

### Report Access by Plan
- Starter: Last 30 days only, basic ticket volume report
- Pro: Last 12 months, all reports except custom dashboards
- Enterprise: Full history, all reports, custom dashboards

### CSAT Surveys
Customer satisfaction surveys are sent automatically after a
ticket is resolved. Customers rate their experience 1-5 stars.
CSAT scores are visible in the Agent Performance report.

## SECTION 9: SECURITY AND DATA

### Data Storage
All NovaDesk data is stored on AWS servers in the US-East
region by default. Enterprise customers can request EU or
APAC region storage.

### Data Retention
Active account data is retained indefinitely. Cancelled
account data is retained for 60 days then permanently deleted.
Agents can export all ticket data before cancellation from
Settings > Data > Export.

### SSO (Single Sign-On)
SSO is available on Enterprise plan only. Supports SAML 2.0
and OAuth 2.0. Setup requires IT admin access and takes
approximately 2 business days to configure with NovaDesk
support team.

### Two-Factor Authentication
2FA is available on all plans. Can be enforced company-wide
by admins from Settings > Security > Enforce 2FA.

## SECTION 10: TROUBLESHOOTING

### Emails Not Creating Tickets
Check that your support email is correctly forwarded to your
NovaDesk inbox address. Go to Settings > Channels > Email to
find your unique forwarding address. Also check your spam
filters are not blocking NovaDesk confirmation emails.

### Live Chat Widget Not Showing
Ensure the widget code is placed before the closing </body>
tag on your website. Clear your browser cache. Check that
your domain is whitelisted in Settings > Channels > Live Chat
> Allowed Domains.

### Agent Not Receiving Tickets
Check the agent's availability status — tickets are not
assigned to agents with Away status. Also verify the agent
is included in the assignment rules under Settings > Automation.

### Cannot Add More Agents
If you cannot add more agents, you have reached your plan
limit. Upgrade your plan to add more agents. Starter plan
maximum is 3 agents, Pro plan maximum is 15 agents.
